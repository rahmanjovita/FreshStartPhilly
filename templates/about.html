Who We Are

FreshStartPhilly: A Pathway to a Brighter Future

At FreshStartPhilly, we recognize the struggles that many formerly incarcerated individuals face when reintegrating into society. The road to rebuilding your life after incarceration is not always easy, and access to critical resources can often feel out of reach. That's why we created FreshStartPhilly, a platform to help guide you towards the support and services you need to thrive in your community.

Our mission is simple: to empower individuals who have been incarcerated by providing easy access to the resources that will help them successfully re-enter society. Whether you’re seeking food, housing, mental health support, job opportunities, or educational programs, FreshStartPhilly connects you with the right services in Philadelphia, so you don’t have to navigate this journey alone.

How We Help You

Navigating the vast number of services available can feel overwhelming. With FreshStartPhilly, you can quickly and easily find the support you need with just a few clicks. Here’s how we make it easy for you:

  • User-Friendly Interface: Our platform is designed with you in mind. Simply enter your location and select the areas where you need assistance – whether it’s food, housing, mental health care, child care, medical care, job search, or education – and we’ll do the rest.
  • Comprehensive Resources: FreshStartPhilly provides you with a list of local resources and services specific to your needs. You won’t waste time sifting through endless websites; we deliver the information you need right at your fingertips.

A Supportive Community for Your Journey

Fresh Start Philly is more than just a resource platform – we’re a community. We believe in second chances and are dedicated to ensuring that formerly incarcerated individuals have the support they need to reintegrate successfully. Our platform is built on the belief that everyone, regardless of their past, deserves a chance to succeed and find happiness.

We are committed to breaking down the barriers that keep people from accessing the resources they need, and we're here to make sure you have the tools to navigate this new chapter of your life with confidence.

Take the First Step Today

The journey to a new beginning starts with one small step, and Fresh Start Philly is here to guide you. Start by entering your zip code or allowing the platform to access your location, select the resources you need, and we’ll provide you with a tailored list of local services that can help.

You don’t have to do this alone. Let us help you find your fresh start. Visit FreshStartPhilly today and begin building the life you deserve.
